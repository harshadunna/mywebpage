Computer science is the study of algorithms, data structures, and computation, encompassing the theory and practice of designing and building software systems. It explores the principles underlying the representation, storage, and processing of information, with applications ranging from software development to artificial intelligence. Data science, a subset of computer science, focuses specifically on extracting insights and knowledge from data through statistical and computational methods, employing techniques like machine learning and data analysis to uncover meaningful patterns and trends.
